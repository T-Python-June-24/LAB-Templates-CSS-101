Assets Management


Assets management involves operating, maintaining, upgrading, and disposing of assets in the most cost-effective manner, considering all costs, risks, and performance attributes.

Category Description
Tangible Assets Physical assets like machinery, equipment, vehicles, etc.
Intangible Assets Non-physical assets like patents, trademarks, copyrights, etc.
Financial Assets Assets like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, etc., that have monetary value.
Current Assets Assets expected to be converted into cash or consumed within a year.
Fixed Assets Long-term assets like buildings, land, machinery, etc., used in production.
